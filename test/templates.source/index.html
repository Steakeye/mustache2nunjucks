Evidence select

A user passing through I.V is asked questions based on information HMRC hold on them. They are given the choice of records and asked to choose one so HMRC knows what to ask them.

Once successful they are redirected to the service url that was passed to the I.V prototype.

Single evidence item and single question journey (CL100)

A single question based on information HMRC hold on the user.

Single evidence item and two question journey (CL200)

Two questions based on information HMRC hold on the user.

Includes the designatory details page which may or may not be required by an individual service.

Two question journey

Two evidence items and two question journey (CL300)

A user passing through I.V is asked to select two evidence options on which they will be asked a question on each based on information HMRC hold on them.

Two evidence items and two questions

No evidence

People without records stored in HMRC or HMPO and have asserted they have no UK passport are pushed to answer questions provided by a commercial credit verification company. If they pass all the questions returned they will be given CL200.

Integrating an external service

The IV prototype can be "sandwiched" by another <service> prototype by linking to an IV page and providing the query parameters url and failUrl

Example

http://iv-prototype.herokuapp.com/redirect/display-url?url=http://www.success-url.com&failUrl=http://www.fail-url.com
