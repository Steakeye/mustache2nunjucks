Apply for Marriage Allowance

Apply to transfer £1,060 of your personal allowance to your husband, wife or civil partner. This reduces their tax by £212.

You’ll need:

  • you and your partner’s National Insurance numbers
  • a way of proving your identity (e.g bank account number, P60)

You have to be the lower earner to make the transfer.
